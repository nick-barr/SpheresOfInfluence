Spheres of Influence

This is a data simulation meant to visualize how closely connected we are to one another. Individuals can reach farther than the immediate surrounding they interact with. While this particular simulation is an example of COVID-19, this also a visual metaphor representing ideas and how our interactions reach one another. Ultimately, I hope this demonstration gives you an understanding of our own sphere of influence.
